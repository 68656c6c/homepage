When playing with a host, certain DMs enforce house rules to make it less headache inducing or certain things that you discover over time, that you would like to use.

Here are the ones we will be using.
## First session
Every session starts off at level 3 when I DM unless otherwise specified, so you can feel like an actual person already. This also means that there are certain items you have to buy, and how much you have.

You start your session with a **FREE** Camping Kit, you can give that to yourself in Pathbuilder 2e by pressing "**Give**" in the **Gear** section of your character sheet, accessing the screen by pressing "**Add Gear**".

You are then given 750 credits to spend on your character to finish their build.

## Hero points
Each *session* starts with 1 hero point, and you get another hero point in the halfway point of the session.

A hero point allows you to reroll your dice, if you fail a save, and you want to try and get out of it, you use your hero point.

>When rerolling, you receive a `+10` unless you rolled above a `10`, so if you rolled a `10`, it would turn your result into a `20`, but an `11` stays `11`.

You will have to specify what type of dice you will be rolling, the attack, hit or save.
