When playing with a host, certain DMs enforce house rules to make it less headache inducing or certain things that you discover over time, that you would like to use.

Current ones for Starfinder 2E are:

Each session starts with a hero point, and you get another hero point in the halfway point.
A hero point allows you to reroll your dice, if you fail a save, and you want to try and get out of it, you use your hero point.
When rerolling, you receive a `+10` unless you rolled above a `10`, so if you rolled a `10`, it would turn your result into a `20`, but an 11 stays `11`.
